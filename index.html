Let me introduce myself

My name is Marco Kruger. I was born in Germany in 1971 and am the father of two adult children. I have always been authentic, honest, and reliable, and I place great importance on quality in everything I do.

My childhood was filled with friendships, adventures, and special experiences. However, I often felt different from others—a feeling that accompanied me throughout my life. I sensed that there was more beyond the visible—a hidden energy, a deeper connection. Without a religious background, I later realized that it was not God but rather my soul and a higher power guiding me.

Until the age of 43, I led a stable life in Germany. Then, everything began to change. Today, this process is known as an "awakening."

Through a unique circumstance, I was introduced to yoga, psychology, prana, reiki, etc. A passion ignited within me—an insatiable thirst for knowledge. At the time, I was a successful entrepreneur, but my newfound world of insights fascinated me so much that I neglected my business. Within a year, I faced bankruptcy and unemployment. But I didn't care at all. The economic downfall was inevitable—yet, for the first time, I was truly free to pursue my deepest interests.

I delved into research, gathered knowledge, and eventually encountered a true medium—someone who could communicate with the unseen world but did not know why. For years, I asked this medium thousands of questions, and she received answers from another dimension that revealed an entirely new world to me.

Over time, the puzzle pieces of knowledge came together. My perception of reality changed drastically. I became vegan, discovered the true diet of humans, and deepened my understanding of fitness, natural healing methods based on the laws of nature, and the body's energy. At the same time, I began to read people, recognize their challenges, and understand them on a deeper level. Spirituality brought clarity and connected all these insights together.

After years of intensive study and practical experience, I knew I had to share this knowledge. Together with the medium, I founded a counseling center in Germany and created several websites to spread awareness. For the first two years, we worked completely free of charge, helping thousands of people facing life challenges.

But my path led me further. A deep inner feeling pulled me to the United States—a decision that once again transformed my life.

Today, I am here to share my knowledge with those who are ready to transform their lives. With those who seek clarity, health, and true happiness.
